Pawel Kozlowski enjoys working on both back-end and front-end and has over 15 years of professional experience in software development. Currently working at Amadeus where he takes care of UI-related frameworks and tools. During his career he was fortunate enough to use multitude of technologies, programming languages and platforms but recently focuses on the front-end web development. He is committed to delivering quality software using all the possible help that we can get from automated testing practices.

Pawel believes in free, open source software and is contributor to several front-end frameworks and tools (Karma-runner, AngularJS and others). He is the co-author of the book "Mastering Web Application Development with AngularJS". When not coding, you can find him speaking at various software development conferences.
